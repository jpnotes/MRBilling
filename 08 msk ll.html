Both left and right of the same region can be scanned on the one day with the second side attracting a lesser fee from Medicare.
Right and left sides are treated as separate regions when considering the nuber of eligible Medicare rebates over a given time. eg. Right knee can be claimed 3 times and left knee can also be claimed 3 times in the same 12 month period.
